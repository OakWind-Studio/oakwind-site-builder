# Niche: Restaurant

Covers restaurants, taco shops, taquerias, BBQ joints, cafés, bakeries, food trucks, sushi bars, pizza shops, and any food service business.

---

## Required Page Elements

- **Menu display** with prices — this is the #1 reason people visit restaurant websites
- **Hours and location** prominently above the fold
- **Food photography** as hero content (the single most important visual element)
- **Online ordering integration point** — even as a "Coming Soon" or link to third-party
- **Phone number** for reservations/takeout, click-to-call
- **Google rating and review count** in first viewport
- **Address with map link** (Google Maps deeplink)

## Trust Signals

- Real food photos (never stock food — it destroys trust)
- Owner/family story ("Family-owned since 2003")
- Google rating badge
- "Locally owned" / community connection
- Health department grade (if A/excellent)
- Specific review quotes about specific dishes

## Section Priority Order

1. Hero (food photo + name + one-liner + hours)
2. Trust strip (rating, years, "family-owned")
3. Menu highlights or full menu
4. About / story (owner, family, origin)
5. Reviews (dish-specific quotes)
6. Location + hours + ordering
7. Contact / footer

## Sub-Niche Variation

### Taco Shops / Taquerias
- **Color:** Vibrant, warm — terracotta, golden yellow, chile red, lime green, deep orange
- **Typography:** Script or handwritten display font + clean sans body. Playful, not corporate
- **Hero:** Full-screen food photo or video of food prep
- **Mood:** Festive, authentic, family, street-food energy
- **Section patterns:** Menu rail (horizontal scroll), gallery of dishes
- **Avoid:** Corporate/sterile layouts, blue/purple tones, stock photos

### BBQ Joints
- **Color:** Dark/smoky — charcoal, deep red, burnt orange, cream/tan
- **Typography:** Slab serif or heavy condensed display. Bold, rugged
- **Hero:** Smoker/pit photo, meat close-up with smoke
- **Mood:** Rustic, proud, pit-master craft
- **Section patterns:** Large food photos between sections, menu with prices
- **Avoid:** Delicate/minimal aesthetics, pastels, thin fonts

### Sushi Bars / Japanese
- **Color:** Minimal — black, white, natural wood, single red or gold accent
- **Typography:** Elegant serif or minimal sans. Restrained
- **Hero:** Clean food photography, negative space
- **Mood:** Refined, zen, precision
- **Section patterns:** Grid with generous spacing, single-item features
- **Avoid:** Cluttered layouts, loud colors, script fonts

### Cafés / Bakeries
- **Color:** Warm pastels — blush, cream, sage, latte, cinnamon
- **Typography:** Rounded sans or friendly serif. Approachable
- **Hero:** Interior ambiance or signature item close-up
- **Mood:** Cozy, inviting, neighborhood gathering spot
- **Section patterns:** Instagram-style photo grid, daily specials
- **Avoid:** Dark/moody themes, industrial aesthetics

### Pizza Shops
- **Color:** Red, green, cream/tan, warm wood tones
- **Typography:** Bold sans or Italian-inspired display. Fun, direct
- **Hero:** Pizza in action — pull, slice, oven
- **Mood:** Fast, friendly, "best slice in town"
- **Section patterns:** Menu with combo deals, delivery info prominent
- **Avoid:** Overly refined/luxury aesthetics

## Style DNA Constraints

| Dimension | Preferred | Avoid |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Layout (1) | Full-Width (1B), Bento (1D) | Single Column (1E) |
| Color (2) | Varies by sub-niche — see above | High Contrast B&W (2E) for most |
| Typography (3) | Script (3C) for taquerias, Slab (3D) for BBQ, Serif (3A) for upscale, Condensed (3E) for pizza | — |
| Hero (4) | Full-Screen Image (4A), Video (4D) | Text-Centric (4C) |
| Animation (5) | Subtle (5B) to Micro (5D) | Full Cinematic (5F) — overkill for food |
| Nav (6) | Transparent Overlay (6B), Sticky (6A) | Sidebar (6E) |
| Sections (7) | Zigzag (7A), Card Grid (7B) | Before/After (7F) |
| Imagery (8) | Lifestyle/Candid (8B), Professional (8A) | Geometric (8F) |
