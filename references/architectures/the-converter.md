# The Converter

**Best for:** home-services, HVAC, pressure-washing
**Personality:** direct, trustworthy, urgent, no-nonsense
**Tier:** 1-2
**Section count:** 6-7
**Animation budget:** 2 scroll-driven effects max

## Emotional Arc
- **Arrival:** Relief — "These guys can fix my problem"
- **Building:** Confidence — "They're legit, fast, and affordable"
- **Decision:** Urgency — "I'm calling right now"

## Section Order + Visual Weight

| # | Section | Recipe Category | Weight | Spacing |
|---|---------|----------------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Hero (phone + offer) | heroes | HERO | hero |
| 2 | Trust proof (licenses, reviews) | trust-proof | WHISPER | tight |
| 3 | Services grid | services | STANDARD | standard |
| 4 | CTA break #1 (call now) | cta-breaks | HERO | standard |

## Rhythm Pattern
HERO → w → s → HERO → s → HERO → w

## Required Sections
- Hero (phone number massive, clear offer/hook)
- Trust proof (licensed, insured, years, review count)
- Services (clear list with pricing hints or "free estimate")
- CTA break #1 (schedule / call — first conversion point)
- CTA break #2 (urgency: "Same-day service" / "Limited spots")
- Contact (phone, form, service area)
- Footer (phone number repeated, OakWind credit)

## Optional Sections
- Before/after gallery
- Pricing table or packages
- FAQ (objection handling)
- Service area map
- Financing / payment options
- Seasonal offer banner

## Signature Characteristic
Three CTA touchpoints ensure the phone number is never more than one scroll away. The phone number is rendered massive in the hero — this architecture prioritizes conversion over aesthetics, with every section engineered to reduce friction to calling.
