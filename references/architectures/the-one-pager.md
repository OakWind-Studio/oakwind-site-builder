# The One-Pager

**Best for:** food truck, solo operator, mobile service
**Personality:** punchy, efficient, friendly, scrappy
**Tier:** 1
**Section count:** 4-5
**Animation budget:** 1 scroll-driven effect max

## Emotional Arc
- **Arrival:** Clarity — "I instantly know what this is"
- **Building:** Trust — "Real reviews, real work"
- **Decision:** Easy — "One tap to call or find them"

## Section Order + Visual Weight

| # | Section | Recipe Category | Weight | Spacing |
|---|---------|----------------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Hero (name + CTA + vibe) | heroes | HERO | hero |
| 2 | Services / menu | services | STANDARD | tight |
| 3 | Reviews or about | testimonials / about | STANDARD | tight |
| 4 | CTA break (call/find us) | cta-breaks | HERO | standard |
| 5 | Footer | footers | WHISPER | tight |

## Rhythm Pattern
HERO → s → s → HERO → w

## Required Sections
- Hero (business name, what they do, phone/location, one strong image)
- Services (compact list or simple grid — no fluff)
- CTA break (phone number, location/hours, action button)
- Footer (essentials only + OakWind credit)

## Optional Sections
- Reviews (3 max, brief)
- About (one paragraph, photo optional)
- Schedule / hours widget
- Social media links

## Signature Characteristic
Brevity is the entire identity — every word earns its place, every section serves a direct purpose. The page loads fast, communicates instantly, and gets out of the way. Perfect for businesses where the phone call IS the conversion, not the website.
