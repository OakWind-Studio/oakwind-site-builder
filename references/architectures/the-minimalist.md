# The Minimalist

**Best for:** premium med-spa, luxury dental, boutique law
**Personality:** restrained, luxurious, intentional, elevated
**Tier:** 3-4
**Section count:** 5-6
**Animation budget:** 3 scroll-driven effects max

## Emotional Arc
- **Arrival:** Intrigue — "This feels expensive"
- **Building:** Aspiration — "This is the level of care I deserve"
- **Decision:** Exclusivity — "I want to be their client"

## Section Order + Visual Weight

| # | Section | Recipe Category | Weight | Spacing |
|---|---------|----------------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Hero (statement) | heroes | HERO | hero |
| 2 | Services (curated) | services | WHISPER | generous |
| 3 | About or reviews | about / testimonials | HERO | generous |
| 4 | Gallery / results | gallery | WHISPER | generous |
| 5 | CTA break | cta-breaks | HERO | generous |
| 6 | Footer | footers | WHISPER | generous |

## Rhythm Pattern
HERO → w → HERO → w → HERO → w

## Required Sections
- Hero (single bold statement, cinematic image or clean type)
- Services (3-4 max, curated not comprehensive)
- Reviews or About (one strong section, not both fighting for space)
- CTA break (elegant invitation, not urgency)
- Footer (minimal, refined)

## Optional Sections
- Single featured testimonial (not carousel)
- Results gallery (small, curated)
- Team / practitioner spotlight
- Booking widget

## Signature Characteristic
Each section occupies nearly the full viewport, with restraint as the defining design principle. What's NOT on the page matters as much as what is — the whitespace, the limited color, the single-statement headlines all signal premium positioning through deliberate absence.
