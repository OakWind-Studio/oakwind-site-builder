# The Storyteller

**Best for:** restaurant, barber, any strong origin story
**Personality:** warm, authentic, narrative, personal
**Tier:** 2-3
**Section count:** 7-9
**Animation budget:** 4 scroll-driven effects max

## Emotional Arc
- **Arrival:** Curiosity — "There's a story here"
- **Building:** Connection — "I feel like I know these people"
- **Decision:** Loyalty — "This is MY place"

## Rhythm Pattern
HERO → w → s → s → w → HERO → s → w → s

## Required Sections
- Hero (mood-setting, atmospheric imagery)
- About — sticky-scroll variant (the signature moment)
- Services / offerings (woven into the narrative)
- Reviews (feel like testimonials from friends)
- CTA break (invitation tone, not urgency)
- Contact (directions, hours, warmth)
- Footer (story continues — tagline, philosophy)

## Optional Sections
- Timeline / history
- Team profiles with personality
- Events / specials
- Photo journal / behind-the-scenes
- Press mentions / features
- Community involvement

## Signature Characteristic
A sticky-scroll about section creates the emotional centerpiece — the founder's story unfolds as you scroll, with parallax images and text that builds genuine connection. The entire page feels like being told a story, not sold a service.
