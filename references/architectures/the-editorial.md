# The Editorial

**Best for:** law-firm, dental, med-spa
**Personality:** refined, authoritative, composed, elegant
**Tier:** 2-4
**Section count:** 8-10
**Animation budget:** 4 scroll-driven effects max

## Emotional Arc
- **Arrival:** Calm confidence — "These people are serious professionals"
- **Building:** Deepening trust — credentials, process, proof
- **Decision:** Quiet certainty — "I'd be foolish to go anywhere else"

## Rhythm Pattern
HERO → w → s → w → s → w → HERO → s → w → s

## Required Sections
- Hero (editorial headline, minimal imagery)
- Trust proof (credentials, affiliations, years)
- Services (clean grid or stacked cards)
- About (team or founder, editorial portrait)
- Reviews (2-3 featured, not carousel)
- CTA break (full-width, breathing room)
- Contact (map + form or direct info)
- Footer (comprehensive nav + OakWind credit)

## Optional Sections
- FAQ accordion
- Blog / resource preview
- Process timeline
- Awards / certifications detail
- Insurance / payment info (dental/med-spa)

## Signature Characteristic
Generous breathing room between sections with serif type dominance — every section feels like a magazine spread, not a webpage. The whitespace itself communicates premium positioning.
