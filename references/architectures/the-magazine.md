# The Magazine

**Best for:** realtor, home-builder, restaurant
**Personality:** dynamic, curated, sophisticated, engaging
**Tier:** 2-3
**Section count:** 7-9
**Animation budget:** 4 scroll-driven effects max

## Emotional Arc
- **Arrival:** Engagement — "There's a lot to explore here"
- **Building:** Discovery — "Every section reveals something new"
- **Decision:** Trust — "They're thorough AND stylish"

## Section Order + Visual Weight

| # | Section | Recipe Category | Weight | Spacing |
|---|---------|----------------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Hero (split or layered) | heroes | HERO | hero |

| 3 | Featured listing / project | gallery | WHISPER | tight |
| 4 | About / team | about | STANDARD | standard |

| 6 | Gallery grid | gallery | STANDARD | standard |

## Rhythm Pattern
HERO → s → w → s → HERO → s → w → s → w

## Required Sections
- Hero (split-screen or editorial layout)
- Services in bento grid (mixed-size cards, not uniform)
- Gallery (varied image sizes, magazine layout)
- About (editorial portrait + bio)
- Reviews (integrated into the flow, not isolated)
- CTA break (consultation / tour / tasting)
- Contact (form + direct info)
- Footer (comprehensive, magazine-style)

## Optional Sections
- Featured listings / projects spotlight
- Stats / by-the-numbers bar
- Awards / press mentions
- Blog / article previews
- Neighborhood / area guide
- Video walkthrough embed

## Signature Characteristic
Mixed-size sections and bento-grid layouts create editorial variety — no two sections feel the same shape. The visual rhythm keeps the eye engaged like flipping through a high-end magazine, with each "spread" offering a different content format.
