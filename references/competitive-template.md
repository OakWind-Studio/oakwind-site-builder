---

## How to Use This Template

1. **During Stage 1 Research:** Google "[niche] [city]" and analyze the top 3 organic results
2. **Fill out one template per competitor** — be specific, not generic
3. **Identify patterns** — what do ALL competitors have in common? That's the sea of sameness
4. **Find the gap** — what's missing from every competitor? That's our opportunity
5. **Write the talking point** — this is what you say when showing the mockup on the iPad
