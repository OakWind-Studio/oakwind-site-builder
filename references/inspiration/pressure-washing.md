# Pressure Washing — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Premium Residential Pressure Washing
**URL:** https://example-pressure-residential.com
**What makes it premium:** Dramatic before/after sliders for driveways, siding, decks, service area map with neighborhood coverage, seasonal cleaning schedule guide, package pricing with square footage tiers
**Technique to borrow:** Before/after sliders for each surface type — driveway, siding, deck, roof — the transformation sells itself

## 2. Commercial Property Washing Service
**URL:** https://example-pressure-commercial.com
**What makes it premium:** Fleet and equipment showcase with professional photography, recurring maintenance plan cards, compliance certifications (EPA, water reclamation), commercial client logo bar, property type selector
**Technique to borrow:** Property type selector in hero — restaurant, retail, warehouse, HOA — visitors self-identify their need immediately

## 3. Soft Wash & Roof Cleaning Specialist
**URL:** https://example-pressure-softwash.com
**What makes it premium:** Educational approach explaining soft wash vs pressure wash, roof cleaning process visualization, damage prevention messaging, insurance and warranty information, algae/moss removal gallery
**Technique to borrow:** Educational soft wash vs pressure wash comparison — positions them as knowledgeable, not just a guy with a hose

## 4. Full-Service Exterior Cleaning
**URL:** https://example-pressure-exterior.com
**What makes it premium:** Service menu with icons for each surface type, same-day scheduling emphasis, weather-aware booking, bundled service discounts, neighborhood group rate section
**Technique to borrow:** Service menu with surface-type icons — house wash, driveway, fence, gutter, deck each get a dedicated icon and description

## 5. Eco-Friendly Pressure Washing
**URL:** https://example-pressure-eco.com
**What makes it premium:** Green certifications prominent, biodegradable product showcase, water reclamation system explainer, environmental impact stats, pet-and-plant-safe messaging
**Technique to borrow:** Environmental impact stats — gallons of water saved, biodegradable products used — differentiates from competitors

## 6. HOA & Multi-Property Washing
**URL:** https://example-pressure-hoa.com
**What makes it premium:** Community-scale before/after galleries, property manager testimonials, multi-unit pricing calculator, scheduling coordination section, curb appeal ROI statistics
**Technique to borrow:** Curb appeal ROI statistics — "pressure washing increases home value by X%" with source citations
