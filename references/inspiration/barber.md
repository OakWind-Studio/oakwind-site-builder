# Barber — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Classic Gentleman's Barbershop
**URL:** https://www.personsofinterestbklyn.com
**What makes it premium:** Brooklyn institution established 2010 with multiple locations (Williamsburg, Fort Greene, Carroll Gardens). Dark atmospheric design with warm tungsten lighting feel. Also operates Altered States wellness offering massage, facials, and wellness rituals.
**Technique to borrow:** Dark atmospheric photography with warm tungsten lighting — makes a barbershop feel like a speakeasy

## 2. Modern Fade & Style Studio
**URL:** https://glossgenius.com/blog/barber-website
**What makes it premium:** Curated showcase of 12 best barber websites featuring Steven Tabach (bold black background highlighting content, seasoned barbers in still images, gallery browsing) and other modern studios with high-contrast portfolios organized by cut style.
**Technique to borrow:** Portfolio grid organized by cut style (fades, tapers, designs) — lets clients browse by what they want

## 3. Premium Grooming Lounge
**URL:** https://www.sitebuilderreport.com/inspiration/barber-barbershop-websites
**What makes it premium:** 40+ barbershop website examples including premium grooming lounges with spa-like aesthetics, services menus styled like restaurant menus (service name, description, duration, price), product line showcases, and membership programs.
**Technique to borrow:** Services menu styled like a restaurant menu — service name, description, duration, price in elegant typography

## 4. Neighborhood Barber Collective
**URL:** https://thesalonbusiness.com/barbershop-website-designs/
**What makes it premium:** 24 best barbershop designs featuring Gentlemen Barber Clubs (award-winning with beautiful photography, typography, textures in uncluttered layout conveying authentic classic barbershop feel) and other community-focused shops.
**Technique to borrow:** Individual barber profiles with personality — specialty, years of experience, signature cut, a personal quote

## 5. Mobile Barber / House Call Service
**URL:** https://creatingawebsitetoday.com/barbershop-websites/
**What makes it premium:** 13 barbershop website examples with mobile-first designs, booking flow integrations, convenience-focused copy. Features service area visualization and on-location portfolio galleries.
**Technique to borrow:** Service area map with animated radius circle — instantly communicates where they'll travel

## 6. Barbershop & Bar Hybrid
**URL:** https://www.blindbarber.com
**What makes it premium:** Blind Barber — the canonical barbershop-bar hybrid. Full-screen lifestyle images, smooth navigation, earthy tones (cream and gray). Wide layout with generous spacing feels relaxed. More lifestyle brand than regular barbershop. (Note: verify current status — locations may have changed.)
**Technique to borrow:** Split-concept visual design — dividing the page to showcase two experiences (cuts + drinks) in one space
