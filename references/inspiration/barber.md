# Barber — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Classic Gentleman's Barbershop
**URL:** https://example-barber-classic.com
**What makes it premium:** Dark palette with brass/gold accents, vintage-inspired typography with modern layout, atmospheric interior photography with dramatic lighting, animated "Est. 20XX" badge, booking widget prominent above fold
**Technique to borrow:** Dark atmospheric photography with warm tungsten lighting — makes a barbershop feel like a speakeasy

## 2. Modern Fade & Style Studio
**URL:** https://example-barber-modern.com
**What makes it premium:** Bold sans-serif typography, high-contrast black and white with neon accent, portfolio grid of cuts organized by style, barber profile cards with specialties, Instagram-style feed integration
**Technique to borrow:** Portfolio grid organized by cut style (fades, tapers, designs) — lets clients browse by what they want

## 3. Premium Grooming Lounge
**URL:** https://example-barber-grooming.com
**What makes it premium:** Elevated spa-like aesthetic, services menu with duration and pricing, product line showcase, membership/loyalty program section, ambient video background
**Technique to borrow:** Services menu styled like a restaurant menu — service name, description, duration, price in elegant typography

## 4. Neighborhood Barber Collective
**URL:** https://example-barber-collective.com
**What makes it premium:** Community-focused storytelling, team section with personality (each barber's style, favorite cut, years experience), local partnerships showcase, event hosting section
**Technique to borrow:** Individual barber profiles with personality — specialty, years of experience, signature cut, a personal quote

## 5. Mobile Barber / House Call Service
**URL:** https://example-barber-mobile.com
**What makes it premium:** Service area map with animated radius, booking flow with location selection, before/after gallery from on-location cuts, convenience-focused copy, mobile-first design
**Technique to borrow:** Service area map with animated radius circle — instantly communicates where they'll travel

## 6. Barbershop & Bar Hybrid
**URL:** https://example-barber-bar.com
**What makes it premium:** Split-concept design (cuts on one side, drinks on the other), experience-focused photography, event calendar, dark luxurious palette, hand-drawn accent illustrations
**Technique to borrow:** Split-concept visual design — literally dividing the page to showcase two experiences in one space
