# Dental — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Upscale Cosmetic Dentistry Practice
**URL:** https://example-dental-cosmetic.com
**What makes it premium:** Scroll-reveal smile gallery with before/after sliders, editorial whitespace between sections, animated credential badges that count up on scroll, cinematic patient journey video in hero
**Technique to borrow:** Before/after smile gallery with smooth slider interaction

## 2. Boutique Family Dental Studio
**URL:** https://example-dental-family.com
**What makes it premium:** Warm earth-tone palette with sage accents, team photos with lifestyle feel (not clinical), patient testimonials paired with treatment photos, hover-reveal service cards
**Technique to borrow:** Lifestyle-feel team photography treatment — makes dental feel approachable, not sterile

## 3. Modern Orthodontics Practice
**URL:** https://example-ortho-modern.com
**What makes it premium:** Split-screen hero with smile close-up and bold headline, treatment timeline with animated progress indicators, results counter (10,000+ smiles), insurance widget integration
**Technique to borrow:** Animated treatment timeline showing the patient journey from consult to reveal

## 4. Luxury Implant Center
**URL:** https://example-dental-implants.com
**What makes it premium:** Dark mode with gold accents, full-viewport technology showcase section, doctor credentials with scroll-triggered animations, virtual consultation CTA with booking integration
**Technique to borrow:** Technology showcase section — makes advanced equipment feel impressive to patients

## 5. Pediatric Dental Boutique
**URL:** https://example-dental-kids.com
**What makes it premium:** Playful but sophisticated illustration system, parent testimonials with child photos, virtual office tour section, gentle color palette with strategic bright accents
**Technique to borrow:** Illustration system that feels premium rather than cartoonish — thin-line custom icons

## 6. Dental Spa Experience
**URL:** https://example-dental-spa.com
**What makes it premium:** Spa-like atmosphere throughout — soft gradients, ambient imagery, relaxation-focused copy, amenity showcase section, aromatherapy and comfort menu
**Technique to borrow:** Amenity/comfort showcase that repositions dental from clinical to spa experience
