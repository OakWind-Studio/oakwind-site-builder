# Dental — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Upscale Cosmetic Dentistry Practice
**URL:** https://www.grandstreetdental.com
**What makes it premium:** Looks like a high-end design gallery rather than a traditional dental site — abstract shapes, sophisticated photography, stylish color palette. Minimalist design with full-screen header and attractively soft-colored theme.
**Technique to borrow:** Editorial art-direction that repositions dentistry as a lifestyle brand, not a clinical service

## 2. Boutique Family Dental Studio
**URL:** https://www.hellotend.com
**What makes it premium:** Minimalist design with easy-to-read content, high-quality images, engaging video showing their dentistry style, clean design with bright images. Simple appointment booking that feels effortless.
**Technique to borrow:** Lifestyle video in hero that reframes dental visits as modern, approachable experiences — not sterile clinical ones

## 3. Modern Orthodontics Practice
**URL:** https://holycityorthodontics.com
**What makes it premium:** Builds immediate trust by introducing the orthodontist front and center, featuring patient reviews, and showcasing before/after results. Clean, confidence-building layout with treatment journey visualization.
**Technique to borrow:** Before/after results gallery paired with doctor credentials — proves outcomes while building authority

## 4. Luxury Implant Center
**URL:** https://www.rosemontmedia.com/website-design/best-dental-website-designs/
**What makes it premium:** Showcase of premium dental designs with dark/luxury aesthetics, gold accents, full-viewport technology sections, scroll-triggered credential animations. Multiple examples of high-end implant center design patterns.
**Technique to borrow:** Technology showcase section — making advanced equipment (CBCT scanners, digital impressions) feel impressive to patients rather than intimidating

## 5. Pediatric Dental Boutique
**URL:** https://delmain.co/blog/best-pediatric-websites/
**What makes it premium:** Curated collection of the best pediatric dental sites including Acorn Pediatric Dental (modern, SEO-optimized, parent-friendly) and CVD Kids (colorful, child-friendly interface that puts parents at ease).
**Technique to borrow:** Playful-but-premium illustration system — thin-line custom icons and warm colors that feel sophisticated rather than cartoonish

## 6. Dental Spa Experience
**URL:** https://perlsmileco.com
**What makes it premium:** Modern Atlanta practice combining cutting-edge technology with a relaxing, contemporary environment. Sleek lines, subtle color palette, engaging visuals designed for patient comfort. Spa-like digital atmosphere throughout.
**Technique to borrow:** Amenity/comfort showcase that repositions dental from clinical to spa experience — aromatherapy, comfort menu, relaxation-focused copy
