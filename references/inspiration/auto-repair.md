# Auto Repair — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Independent European Auto Specialist
**URL:** https://example-auto-european.com
**What makes it premium:** Vehicle make selector (BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Porsche) in hero, brand-specific certification badges, OEM vs aftermarket parts transparency, loaner vehicle availability, shop tour video
**Technique to borrow:** Vehicle make selector in hero — visitors click their brand and see specialized services, immediately building confidence

## 2. Full-Service Family Auto Shop
**URL:** https://example-auto-family.com
**What makes it premium:** "Meet the mechanics" section with ASE certifications, digital vehicle inspection report preview, maintenance schedule tool by mileage, waiting area amenities showcase, multi-generation family story
**Technique to borrow:** Digital inspection report preview — showing what the customer receives builds transparency and modernizes the shop image

## 3. Performance & Tuning Garage
**URL:** https://example-auto-performance.com
**What makes it premium:** Dark aggressive palette, project build gallery with specs, dyno results with charts, modification packages tiered by performance level, car meet/event section
**Technique to borrow:** Project build gallery with before/after dyno numbers — measurable results tell a compelling story

## 4. Quick Lube & Maintenance Center
**URL:** https://example-auto-quicklube.com
**What makes it premium:** Real-time wait time indicator, service menu with transparent pricing, online appointment scheduling, maintenance reminder signup, fleet/commercial account section
**Technique to borrow:** Real-time wait time indicator — "Current wait: ~15 min" creates transparency and reduces friction to visit

## 5. Collision Repair & Body Shop
**URL:** https://example-auto-collision.com
**What makes it premium:** Insurance company partnership logos, repair process timeline (estimate → repair → paint → QC → delivery), before/after collision repair gallery, rental car coordination section, lifetime warranty badge
**Technique to borrow:** Repair process timeline — demystifies body work and sets clear expectations for anxious car owners

## 6. Tire & Alignment Shop
**URL:** https://example-auto-tires.com
**What makes it premium:** Tire finder tool by vehicle year/make/model, brand logos (Michelin, Goodyear, BFGoodrich), seasonal tire changeover promotions, alignment visualization diagram, price match guarantee badge
**Technique to borrow:** Tire finder by vehicle — interactive tool that feels helpful, not salesy, and captures vehicle data for follow-up
