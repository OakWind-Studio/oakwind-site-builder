# Auto Repair — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Independent European Auto Specialist
**URL:** https://craftsmanautocare.com
**What makes it premium:** Craftsman Auto Care — multiple locations with strong local branding. Professional design with vehicle service focus, trust-building certifications. Specialization positioning that commands higher prices than generic shops.
**Technique to borrow:** Vehicle make selector in hero — visitors click their brand and see specialized services, immediately building confidence

## 2. Full-Service Family Auto Shop
**URL:** https://www.mcleanautorepair.com
**What makes it premium:** McLean Auto Repair — trusted family-owned shop serving Elgin, IL since 1970. ASE-certified mechanics prominently featured. Multi-generation family story woven into the about section. Vehicles serviced section organized by make.
**Technique to borrow:** Digital inspection report preview — showing what the customer receives builds transparency and modernizes the shop image

## 3. Performance & Tuning Garage
**URL:** https://mycodelesswebsite.com/auto-repair-website/
**What makes it premium:** 53 best auto repair website examples featuring performance shops with dark aggressive palettes, project build galleries with specs, dyno results with charts. Modification packages tiered by performance level.
**Technique to borrow:** Project build gallery with before/after dyno numbers — measurable results tell a compelling story

## 4. Quick Lube & Maintenance Center
**URL:** https://westraliacarcare.com.au
**What makes it premium:** Westralia Car Care — hero video virtual tour of the shop with mechanics at work, prominent CTA buttons overlaid to funnel customers. Modern clean design focused on quick service convenience. Multiple locations with consistent branding.
**Technique to borrow:** Real-time wait time indicator or virtual shop tour — "Current wait: ~15 min" creates transparency and reduces friction

## 5. Collision Repair & Body Shop
**URL:** https://freshysites.com/blog/top-auto-repair-websites/
**What makes it premium:** Top 30 auto repair websites featuring AutoCraft (professional clean design emphasizing collision repair, homepage presenting services, safety policies, contact details) and similar shops with insurance partnership logos and repair process timelines.
**Technique to borrow:** Repair process timeline — demystifies body work and sets clear expectations for anxious car owners

## 6. Tire & Alignment Shop
**URL:** https://www.getjobber.com/academy/auto-detailing/auto-detailing-websites/
**What makes it premium:** Professional auto service website examples featuring shops with tire finder tools by vehicle year/make/model, brand logos (Michelin, Goodyear), seasonal changeover promotions, and price match guarantees.
**Technique to borrow:** Tire finder by vehicle — interactive tool that feels helpful, not salesy, and captures vehicle data for follow-up
