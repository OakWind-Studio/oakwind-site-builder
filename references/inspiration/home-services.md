# Home Services — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Premium HVAC Company
**URL:** https://example-hvac-premium.com
**What makes it premium:** Emergency service banner with pulsing phone number, trust badge bar (licensed, insured, BBB A+, 5-star rated), service type cards with seasonal relevance, financing calculator widget, same-day guarantee badge
**Technique to borrow:** Emergency service banner with pulsing/animated phone number — creates urgency and makes the CTA impossible to miss

## 2. Full-Service Plumbing Company
**URL:** https://example-plumbing-full.com
**What makes it premium:** 24/7 availability emphasis, service area map with coverage zones, pricing transparency section (trip charge, diagnostic fee), video testimonials from homeowners, before/after pipe repair gallery
**Technique to borrow:** Pricing transparency section — showing the diagnostic fee and trip charge upfront builds immediate trust

## 3. Electrical Contractor
**URL:** https://example-electrical-contractor.com
**What makes it premium:** Safety-first messaging, license and certification badges prominent, service categories (residential, commercial, emergency), project gallery with professional installation photos, energy savings calculator
**Technique to borrow:** License and certification badge display — electrical work requires trust, and credentials displayed prominently address that

## 4. Roofing Company
**URL:** https://example-roofing-company.com
**What makes it premium:** Storm damage CTA with weather integration, free inspection scheduling, insurance claims assistance section, material comparison (shingle types), drone inspection photo gallery
**Technique to borrow:** Storm damage urgency CTA — "Recent hail in [area]? Free inspection" with weather-triggered messaging

## 5. Handyman & General Contractor
**URL:** https://example-handyman-pro.com
**What makes it premium:** Service checklist with 30+ items organized by room, online booking with photo upload, flat-rate pricing display, "No job too small" messaging, customer review carousel with project photos
**Technique to borrow:** Service checklist organized by room (kitchen, bathroom, exterior) — helps homeowners realize how many things they need done

## 6. Pest Control Service
**URL:** https://example-pest-control.com
**What makes it premium:** Pest identifier tool with photos, seasonal pest calendar, treatment plan options (one-time vs recurring), family and pet safety assurance section, satisfaction guarantee prominent
**Technique to borrow:** Seasonal pest calendar showing which pests are active when — educational content that drives seasonal bookings
