# General Premium — Cross-Niche Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks when building for niches without their own inspiration file. These techniques work universally across local businesses.

## 1. Award-Winning Small Business Website
**URL:** https://example-premium-smallbiz.com
**What makes it premium:** Micro-interactions on every interactive element, scroll-triggered entrance animations with staggered timing, custom cursor states, noise texture overlay for depth, fluid typography scaling between mobile and desktop
**Technique to borrow:** Scroll-triggered staggered entrance animations — elements don't appear all at once, they cascade in reading order

## 2. Boutique Service Provider
**URL:** https://example-premium-boutique.com
**What makes it premium:** Editorial whitespace (40%+ of viewport is breathing room), limited color palette (2 colors + neutrals), oversized serif headlines with tight body copy, single-column layout with intentional asymmetric breaks
**Technique to borrow:** Editorial whitespace ratio — 40%+ empty space signals premium positioning without adding content

## 3. Modern Local Brand
**URL:** https://example-premium-localbrand.com
**What makes it premium:** Custom illustrated icons instead of stock icon libraries, hand-crafted color palette with unexpected accent, smooth page transitions between sections, bespoke loading animation, branded scrollbar
**Technique to borrow:** Custom illustrated icons that match the brand personality — thin-line, consistent stroke width, unique to the business

## 4. High-End Service Business
**URL:** https://example-premium-highend.com
**What makes it premium:** Video hero with elegant text overlay, testimonials presented as editorial quotes (large serif, attribution below), process section with numbered steps and connecting lines, footer as a design moment (not afterthought)
**Technique to borrow:** Footer as design moment — branded background, considered typography, the page ending feels intentional

## 5. Tech-Forward Local Business
**URL:** https://example-premium-techforward.com
**What makes it premium:** Dark mode with gradient accent glows, glassmorphism cards, smooth scroll with momentum, parallax depth layers, animated stats counters, modern geometric patterns
**Technique to borrow:** Gradient glow accents on dark backgrounds — colored light bleeds behind cards and sections create depth and atmosphere

## 6. Community-Rooted Business
**URL:** https://example-premium-community.com
**What makes it premium:** Local photography (recognizable landmarks and streets), community involvement section, team personality showcase, warm authentic tone, neighborhood-specific copy, local partnership badges
**Technique to borrow:** Local photography integration — using recognizable local landmarks grounds the business in community trust
