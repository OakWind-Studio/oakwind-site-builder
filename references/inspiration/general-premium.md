# General Premium — Cross-Niche Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks when building for niches without their own inspiration file. These techniques work universally across local businesses.

## 1. Award-Winning Small Business Website
**URL:** https://onenine.com/modern-web-design-examples/
**What makes it premium:** Curated collection of modern web design examples featuring micro-interactions on every interactive element, scroll-triggered entrance animations with staggered timing, noise texture overlays for depth, and fluid typography scaling between mobile and desktop.
**Technique to borrow:** Scroll-triggered staggered entrance animations — elements don't appear all at once, they cascade in reading order

## 2. Boutique Service Provider
**URL:** https://knapsackcreative.com/blog/web-design/best-small-business-websites
**What makes it premium:** 15 best small business websites featuring Lori May Interiors (gold-and-dark-green palette, stunning full-width hero, elegant logo and structured navigation conveying premium) and Solo Salon (muted sage-green hero with bold serif typography making confident statement).
**Technique to borrow:** Editorial whitespace ratio — 40%+ empty space signals premium positioning without adding content

## 3. Modern Local Brand
**URL:** https://www.framer.com/blog/small-business-website-examples/
**What makes it premium:** 15 small business website examples for design inspiration featuring custom illustrated icons, hand-crafted color palettes with unexpected accents, smooth page transitions, branded scrollbar, and bespoke loading animations.
**Technique to borrow:** Custom illustrated icons that match the brand personality — thin-line, consistent stroke width, unique to the business

## 4. High-End Service Business
**URL:** https://www.dllstudios.com/post/7-top-small-business-website-examples-to-inspire-you-in-2026
**What makes it premium:** 7 top small business examples featuring video hero with elegant text overlay, testimonials presented as editorial quotes (large serif, attribution below), numbered process sections with connecting lines, and footers as design moments.
**Technique to borrow:** Footer as design moment — branded background, considered typography, the page ending feels intentional not afterthought

## 5. Tech-Forward Local Business
**URL:** https://creativethemes.com/blocksy/blog/small-business-websites-examples/
**What makes it premium:** 34 small business website examples including dark mode designs with gradient accent glows, glassmorphism cards, smooth scroll with momentum, parallax depth layers, animated stats counters, and modern geometric patterns.
**Technique to borrow:** Gradient glow accents on dark backgrounds — colored light bleeds behind cards and sections create depth and atmosphere

## 6. Community-Rooted Business
**URL:** https://www.neo.space/blog/small-business-website
**What makes it premium:** 10 stunning small business designs featuring local photography (recognizable landmarks and streets), community involvement sections, team personality showcases, warm authentic tone, neighborhood-specific copy, and local partnership badges.
**Technique to borrow:** Local photography integration — using recognizable local landmarks grounds the business in community trust
