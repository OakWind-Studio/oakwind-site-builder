# Home Builder — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Custom Luxury Home Builder
**URL:** https://www.tollbrothers.com
**What makes it premium:** Nation's leading luxury home builder — immersive visual experience with expansive high-resolution photography, elegant parallax scrolling creating depth. "Design Your Own Home" interactive tool lets buyers personalize floor plans. Nearly 500 design options.
**Technique to borrow:** Scroll-driven project reveal — exterior first, then interior rooms unfold as you scroll, with parallax depth

## 2. Modern Spec Home Developer
**URL:** https://comradeweb.com/blog/best-roofing-websites/
**What makes it premium:** Curated showcase featuring NoCO Custom Homes (excels at location-specific branding emphasizing Colorado living) and Cornerstone Homes (bold contemporary aesthetic with dramatic full-screen luxury mountain property imagery). Clean minimalist navigation with sophisticated typography.
**Technique to borrow:** Available properties section with status indicators (Available, Under Contract, Sold) — creates urgency

## 3. Renovation & Addition Specialist
**URL:** https://www.bolsterbuilt.com
**What makes it premium:** Before/after slider comparisons for renovation projects, project type categorization (kitchen, bath, full gut, addition), transparent pricing calculator, detailed project case studies with timelines and budgets.
**Technique to borrow:** Before/after slider galleries for renovation projects with project scope and budget breakdowns inline — the transformation IS the sales pitch

## 4. Farmhouse & Ranch Builder
**URL:** https://www.heritagerestorations.com
**What makes it premium:** Warm textural design with reclaimed wood aesthetics carried into the UI. Land-to-home storytelling where each project reads like a magazine feature with land, materials, and family story woven together. Barn/farmhouse gallery organized by style.
**Technique to borrow:** Editorial storytelling approach — from empty lot to finished home in a scroll sequence, each project a narrative

## 5. Green / Sustainable Builder
**URL:** https://freshysites.com/web-design-development/top-25-home-builders-websites/
**What makes it premium:** Curated collection featuring builders with nature-forward palettes, energy savings calculators, certification badges (LEED, Energy Star, Net Zero), and sustainability philosophy woven into design. Per-home energy performance cards.
**Technique to borrow:** Energy savings visualization — projected monthly savings compared to standard construction with certification badges

## 6. Multi-Family & Townhome Developer
**URL:** https://www.brookfieldresidential.com
**What makes it premium:** Community-first design with amenity grids, interactive floor plan explorers with 3D walkthroughs, neighborhood lifestyle content. Filterable search by community, price, and beds. Togglable furniture layouts with per-room square footage.
**Technique to borrow:** Amenity bento grid with mixed-size cards — pool gets hero treatment, mailroom gets thumbnail
