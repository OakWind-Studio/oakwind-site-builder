# Home Builder — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Custom Luxury Home Builder
**URL:** https://example-builder-luxury.com
**What makes it premium:** Cinematic project galleries with scroll-driven reveals, process timeline from design to move-in, material/finish showcase section, architectural detail close-ups
**Technique to borrow:** Scroll-driven project reveal — exterior first, then interior rooms unfold as you scroll down

## 2. Modern Spec Home Developer
**URL:** https://example-builder-spec.com
**What makes it premium:** Clean contemporary design matching the homes, available properties with virtual tours, community master plan visualization, energy efficiency badges
**Technique to borrow:** Available properties section with status indicators (Available, Under Contract, Sold) — creates urgency

## 3. Renovation & Addition Specialist
**URL:** https://example-builder-renovation.com
**What makes it premium:** Before/after project showcases with dramatic sliders, project type selector, budget range guide, permit and timeline transparency
**Technique to borrow:** Before/after slider galleries for renovation projects — the transformation IS the sales pitch

## 4. Farmhouse & Ranch Builder
**URL:** https://example-builder-ranch.com
**What makes it premium:** Warm, textured design with wood and stone accents, land-to-home journey storytelling, acreage/lot integration, rural lifestyle photography
**Technique to borrow:** Land-to-home journey storytelling — from empty lot to finished home in a scroll sequence

## 5. Green / Sustainable Builder
**URL:** https://example-builder-green.com
**What makes it premium:** Nature-forward palette, energy savings calculator, certification badges (LEED, Energy Star), material sourcing transparency, utility cost comparison
**Technique to borrow:** Energy savings visualization — projected monthly savings compared to standard construction

## 6. Multi-Family & Townhome Developer
**URL:** https://example-builder-multifamily.com
**What makes it premium:** Community-focused design, amenity bento grids, floor plan explorer, neighborhood integration section, resident testimonials
**Technique to borrow:** Amenity bento grid with mixed-size cards — pool gets hero treatment, mailroom gets thumbnail
