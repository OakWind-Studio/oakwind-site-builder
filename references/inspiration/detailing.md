# Detailing — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Luxury Auto Detailing Studio
**URL:** https://example-detailing-luxury.com
**What makes it premium:** Dark showroom aesthetic with reflective surfaces, before/after sliders with dramatic reveals, ceramic coating process video, package tier cards with clear deliverables, gallery organized by vehicle type (exotic, SUV, daily driver)
**Technique to borrow:** Before/after sliders with dramatic lighting — the paint correction reveal IS the sales pitch

## 2. Mobile Detailing Service
**URL:** https://example-detailing-mobile.com
**What makes it premium:** Service area map with zip code checker, package comparison table with visual icons, real-time booking calendar, fleet/commercial section for business clients, photo gallery with vehicle make/model tags
**Technique to borrow:** Package comparison table with visual icons for each included service — makes complex packages instantly scannable

## 3. Ceramic Coating & PPF Specialist
**URL:** https://example-detailing-ceramic.com
**What makes it premium:** Technical showcase with product certifications, coating longevity timeline visualization, warranty information with trust badges, installation process step-by-step, branded product partner logos
**Technique to borrow:** Coating longevity timeline — visual showing protection level at 1yr, 3yr, 5yr builds value for premium services

## 4. Classic & Exotic Car Detailing
**URL:** https://example-detailing-exotic.com
**What makes it premium:** Cinematic gallery with exotic car photography, white-glove service messaging, private appointment scheduling, insurance-grade documentation section, client garage showcase
**Technique to borrow:** Cinematic car photography with dramatic angles and studio lighting — elevates detailing to automotive art

## 5. Fleet & Commercial Detailing
**URL:** https://example-detailing-fleet.com
**What makes it premium:** Corporate-clean design, fleet size calculator, recurring service plans, compliance and eco-certifications, client logo bar with recognizable local businesses
**Technique to borrow:** Fleet size calculator that auto-generates a quote estimate — interactive tool that doubles as lead capture

## 6. Paint Correction & Restoration
**URL:** https://example-detailing-restore.com
**What makes it premium:** Process documentation with numbered steps and close-up photography, defect identification guide, 50/50 shots (half corrected, half original), tool and product transparency, satisfaction guarantee badge
**Technique to borrow:** 50/50 correction shots — half the panel corrected, half untouched, photographed under inspection light
