## 1. Luxury Auto Detailing Studio
**URL:** https://esotericdetail.com
**What makes it premium:** ESOTERIC — 50+ skilled automotive enthusiasts, 15+ years in business. Dark showroom aesthetic with reflective surfaces. Paint correction process documentation with dramatic 50/50 shots under inspection lighting. Premium positioning through technical expertise.
**Technique to borrow:** Before/after sliders with dramatic lighting — the paint correction reveal IS the sales pitch

## 2. Mobile Detailing Service
**URL:** https://www.cyberoptik.net/blog/best-auto-detailing-websites-success-secrets/
**What makes it premium:** Curated showcase of 20 best detailing websites featuring mobile service providers with service area maps, zip code checkers, package comparison tables with visual icons, real-time booking calendars, and fleet/commercial sections.
**Technique to borrow:** Package comparison table with visual icons for each included service — makes complex packages instantly scannable

## 3. Ceramic Coating & PPF Specialist
**URL:** https://www.chicagoautopros.com
**What makes it premium:** Chicago Auto Pros — leader in paint protection with sharp layout, clear navigation to detailing/ceramic/PPF/wrap services. Dark aesthetic creating premium feel. XPEL ceramic coating packages with comprehensive tier cards. Technical expertise shines through.
**Technique to borrow:** Coating longevity timeline — visual showing protection level at 1yr, 3yr, 5yr builds value for premium services

## 4. Classic & Exotic Car Detailing

## 5. Fleet & Commercial Detailing
