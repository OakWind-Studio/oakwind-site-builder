# Med-Spa — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Luxury Aesthetic Center
**URL:** https://www.beverlyhillsmedspa.com
**What makes it premium:** Pinnacle of luxury med spa design — refined lotus logo, blush-pink palette radiating elegance. Badges of honor and award seals serve as instant social proof. Tagline "Elevating Existing Beauty" with glamorous, feminine aesthetic throughout.
**Technique to borrow:** Soft gradient backgrounds that shift subtly — creates a spa-like digital atmosphere that patients feel before they visit

## 2. Modern Injection & Filler Studio
**URL:** https://www.glanzaesthetics.com
**What makes it premium:** Above-the-fold hero video grabs attention instantly, well-photographed shots contributing to luxurious feel. Focus on prevention, prejuvenation, and rejuvenation with cutting-edge technology showcase. Full-service menu with clear treatment categories.
**Technique to borrow:** Treatment comparison layout — side-by-side cards comparing Botox vs. filler vs. other options help patients self-select

## 3. Wellness & Regenerative Clinic
**URL:** https://milkandhoneyspa.com
**What makes it premium:** Seamless experience with well-structured layout and inviting design. Double header design keeps navigation visible. Boxed layout organizes services allowing users to browse treatments without extra clicks. Holistic wellness approach woven into every section.
**Technique to borrow:** Treatment journey visualization — what to expect timeline with icons for each phase reduces anxiety

## 4. Celebrity-Style Aesthetics Practice
**URL:** https://www.nassifmdmedspa.com
**What makes it premium:** Celebrity surgeon's med spa with high-contrast design, dramatic before/after reveals, media appearances section (as seen on TV). VIP treatment positioning with premium membership options. Strong personal brand backed by television credibility.
**Technique to borrow:** Dramatic before/after reveal — image wipes on scroll rather than static comparison create engagement

## 5. Medical Skincare Boutique
**URL:** https://www.bwaesthetics.com
**What makes it premium:** Beverly Wilshire Aesthetics — "The Maison of Supreme Skin" positioning. Refined palette of whites, soft blues, warm accents. Product line integration alongside treatment menus. Dewy photography style throughout.
**Technique to borrow:** Skin concern quiz that doubles as a lead funnel — interactive, engaging, data-collecting

## 6. Men's Aesthetics Clinic
**URL:** https://www.lyevbeverlyhills.com
**What makes it premium:** LYEV Beverly Hills — luxury aesthetic and wellness treatments with a design that doesn't default to feminine-coded med spa patterns. Darker, more confident palette communicating sophistication. Results-focused rather than procedure-focused messaging.
**Technique to borrow:** Masculine design approach — proves med-spa sites don't have to be soft pink and rose gold to feel premium
