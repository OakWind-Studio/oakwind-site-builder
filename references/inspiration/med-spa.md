# Med-Spa — Premium Site Inspiration

Reference during Stage 2 for quality benchmarks. Use Firecrawl to scrape 2-3 of these.

## 1. Luxury Aesthetic Center
**URL:** https://example-medspa-luxury.com
**What makes it premium:** Soft gradients (blush to champagne), scroll-reveal treatment galleries, elegant before/after with privacy-conscious presentation, animated treatment process diagrams
**Technique to borrow:** Soft gradient backgrounds that shift subtly as you scroll — creates a spa-like digital atmosphere

## 2. Modern Injection & Filler Studio
**URL:** https://example-medspa-injectables.com
**What makes it premium:** Clean white with rose gold accents, provider credentials with animated badges, treatment comparison tool, results timeline showing healing progression
**Technique to borrow:** Treatment comparison layout — side-by-side cards comparing Botox vs. filler vs. other options

## 3. Wellness & Regenerative Clinic
**URL:** https://example-medspa-wellness.com
**What makes it premium:** Earth-tone palette with sage green, holistic design language, treatment journey visualization, ingredient/technology explainers with elegant diagrams
**Technique to borrow:** Treatment journey visualization — what to expect timeline with icons for each phase

## 4. Celebrity-Style Aesthetics Practice
**URL:** https://example-medspa-celeb.com
**What makes it premium:** High-contrast black and white with gold, dramatic before/after reveals on scroll, provider featured in publications section, VIP membership tier cards
**Technique to borrow:** Dramatic before/after reveal — image wipes on scroll rather than static comparison

## 5. Medical Skincare Boutique
**URL:** https://example-medspa-skincare.com
**What makes it premium:** Soft, dewy photography style, product line integration, skin quiz/consultation funnel, ingredient spotlight sections with molecular illustrations
**Technique to borrow:** Skin concern quiz that doubles as a lead funnel — interactive, engaging, data-collecting

## 6. Men's Aesthetics Clinic
**URL:** https://example-medspa-mens.com
**What makes it premium:** Darker, masculine palette (charcoal, navy, stone), straightforward copy without clinical jargon, discreet testimonials, results-focused rather than procedure-focused
**Technique to borrow:** Masculine design approach — proves med-spa sites don't have to be feminine-coded
