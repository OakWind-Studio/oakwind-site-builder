# Emotional Arc Templates

Every site guides the visitor through three emotional beats. The arc determines section ORDER, not just content — a site for a skeptical audience front-loads proof, while a site for an excited audience front-loads aspiration.

## Per-Niche Arcs

| Niche | Arrival Emotion | Building Emotion | Decision Emotion |
|-------|----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Dental | Nervous → Reassured | Trust (credentials, process transparency, gentle approach) | Confidence ("I trust this dentist with my smile") |
| Restaurant | Hungry → Excited | Excitement (food photos, atmosphere, chef story) | FOMO ("I need to eat here before it's packed") |
| Attorney | Worried → Hopeful | Trust (case results, credentials, process clarity) | Confidence + Urgency ("They can help me, I should call now") |
| Med Spa | Curious → Aspirational | Aspiration (transformation results, luxury experience) | Personal Connection ("This feels right for me") |
| Realtor | Browsing → Engaged | Trust (market knowledge, sold homes, neighborhood expertise) | Confidence ("This agent knows my area") |
| Home Builder | Dreaming → Envisioning | Excitement (portfolio, floor plans, craftsmanship details) | Aspiration ("I can see my family here") |
| Wedding Venue | Dreaming → In Love | Aspiration (photos, testimonials, the feeling of the space) | Personal Connection ("This is where our story begins") |
| Barber | Looking → Found My Guy | Connection (owner story, vibe, community roots) | Personal Connection ("This is my spot") |
| Detailing | Proud → Want Better | Excitement (transformations, before/after, precision) | Aspiration ("My car deserves this") |
| Pressure Washing | Frustrated → Relieved | Trust (results, speed, professionalism) | Urgency ("I want this done now") |
| Home Services | Stressed → Relieved | Trust (speed, availability, credentials, process) | Urgency ("They're available and reliable") |
| Auto Repair | Skeptical → Reassured | Trust (certifications, transparency, fair pricing) | Confidence ("They won't rip me off") |
| Law Firm | Overwhelmed → Empowered | Trust (attorney profiles, case results, firm history) | Confidence ("This firm has the firepower") |

## How Arcs Map to Section Order

### Trust-First Arc (skeptical audience)
Hero (reassurance) → Credentials/Process → Reviews → Services → About → CTA
**Use for:** Dental, Auto Repair, Attorney, Home Services

### Excitement-First Arc (aspirational audience)
Hero (aspiration) → Gallery/Portfolio → Services → Reviews → About → CTA
**Use for:** Restaurant, Wedding Venue, Home Builder, Detailing

### Connection-First Arc (community audience)
Hero (personality) → About/Owner Story → Services → Reviews → Community → CTA
**Use for:** Barber, Restaurant (casual), Med Spa

### Urgency-First Arc (problem-solving audience)
Hero (solution) → Availability/Speed → Services → Reviews → Contact → CTA
**Use for:** Pressure Washing, Home Services, HVAC (emergency)
