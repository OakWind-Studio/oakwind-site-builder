# Niche: Home Services

Covers HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, handyman, pest control, garage door, fencing, painting, and general home repair/improvement services.

---

## Required Page Elements

- **Service list with descriptions** — organized by category, not just a bullet list
- **Phone number** — click-to-call, sticky, impossible to miss. Home services are emergency-driven — someone with a burst pipe at 2am needs to find the number instantly
- **Service area** — neighborhoods, cities, zip codes, or radius. Hyperlocal is everything
- **Google rating and review count** — first viewport
- **Licensed, bonded, and insured badges** — non-negotiable for home services trust
- **"Free estimate" or "Free inspection" CTA** — lowers the commitment barrier
- **Hours / availability** — especially 24/7 or emergency service if applicable
- **Warranty/guarantee** — "satisfaction guaranteed", "1-year workmanship warranty"

## Trust Signals (priority order)

1. Google rating + review count
2. "Licensed, Bonded & Insured" badge
3. Years in business / "Family-owned since XXXX"
4. BBB accreditation
5. Owner name and photo — accountability
6. Specific certifications (EPA, NATE for HVAC, master plumber license)
7. Warranty details
8. Service area specificity
9. "Background-checked technicians" if applicable
10. Brand partnerships (Trane, Rheem, Carrier for HVAC)

## Section Priority Order

1. Hero (what you do + service area + phone + "Free Estimate")
2. Trust strip (rating, years, licensed/insured, emergency availability)
3. Services (organized by category)
4. Why choose us (differentiators, guarantees, process)
5. About (owner story, team, community roots)
6. Reviews (service-specific: "fixed our AC in 2 hours")
7. Service area (map or city/neighborhood list)
8. Contact / estimate form
9. Footer

## Sub-Niche Variation

### HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning)
- **Color:** Blue + orange is classic (cool/warm duality). Red for heating focus, blue for cooling
- **Typography:** Bold sans. Professional but approachable
- **Hero:** Comfortable family at home, or technician at work
- **Urgency:** "AC broken? Same-day repair" — seasonal urgency drives this niche
- **Key sections:** Seasonal tune-up specials, brand partnerships, financing options
- **Certifications:** NATE certified, EPA certified, brand-authorized dealer

### Plumbing
- **Color:** Blue + white (water), green accents (eco/clean)
- **Typography:** Bold, trustworthy sans-serif
- **Hero:** Clean, modern bathroom/kitchen, or technician
- **Urgency:** "Burst pipe? Call now — 24/7 emergency service"
- **Key sections:** Emergency vs. scheduled services, water heater, drain cleaning
- **Trust emphasis:** Licensed master plumber, years of experience

### Roofing
- **Color:** Dark slate/charcoal + bold accent (red, orange, blue)
- **Typography:** Heavy, industrial. Condensed uppercase works well
- **Hero:** Completed roof with blue sky, or aerial/drone shot
- **Trust emphasis:** Licensed, insured, warranty details, "free roof inspection"
- **Key sections:** Roof types (shingle, metal, tile), storm damage, before/after
- **Seasonal:** Hail damage, storm season urgency

### Landscaping
- **Color:** Green palette (sage, emerald, forest) + earth tones (brown, tan, terracotta)
- **Typography:** Clean sans for modern, rounded sans for friendly
- **Hero:** Beautiful completed landscape, lush green lawn, outdoor living space
- **Trust emphasis:** Design portfolio, licensed, years of experience
- **Key sections:** Design vs. maintenance services, seasonal cleanup, irrigation
- **Before/after:** Transformation photos are powerful here

### Electrician
- **Color:** Yellow/amber (electricity association) + dark grey/navy
- **Typography:** Bold sans, professional
- **Hero:** Modern lighting, panel work, or smart home
- **Urgency:** "Electrical emergency? Available 24/7"
- **Trust emphasis:** Licensed master electrician, insured, code compliant
- **Key sections:** Residential vs. commercial, panel upgrades, EV charger installation

### Handyman / General
- **Color:** Earth tones, warm palette. Red/blue for tools association
- **Typography:** Friendly, approachable sans-serif
- **Hero:** Completed project or friendly technician
- **Trust emphasis:** Background-checked, insured, "one call fixes it all"
- **Key sections:** Full service list, "no job too small", pricing transparency

## Style DNA Constraints

| Dimension | Preferred | Avoid |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Layout (1) | Classic Centered (1A), Full-Width (1B) | Bento (1D), Overlap (1F) |
| Color (2) | Light + Vibrant (2A), High Contrast (2E) | Pastel (2F) — home services need to feel capable, not gentle |
| Typography (3) | Bold Sans (3B), Condensed (3E), Slab (3D) | Script (3C), Serif (3A) — too formal for trades |
| Hero (4) | Split (4B), Full-Screen (4A) | Text-Centric (4C), Animated (4F) |
| Animation (5) | Subtle (5B), Micro (5D) | Parallax (5C), Full Cinematic (5F) |
| Nav (6) | Sticky (6A) — phone number always visible | Sidebar (6E), Hamburger desktop (6C) |
| Sections (7) | Card Grid (7B), Zigzag (7A), Process Flow (7C) | Before/After (7F) — except landscaping/roofing |
| Imagery (8) | Professional (8A), Lifestyle (8B) | Illustrations (8C), Geometric (8F) |

## Copy Patterns That Work

- "Keeping [city] homes comfortable since [year]"
- "Licensed. Insured. Guaranteed."
- "One call. Problem solved."
- "24/7 emergency service — we're here when you need us"
- "[Owner] and his team treat every home like their own"
- "Free estimates — no obligation, no pressure"
- "Your neighbors trust us. You will too."
- "[X]+ [city] homes served and counting"

## Anti-Patterns

- Stock photos of models pretending to be tradespeople — feels fake
- Hiding the phone number — emergencies drive this niche
- No service area — if they can't tell if you serve their area, they'll call someone else
- Corporate/enterprise feel — these are local, personal businesses
- No emergency messaging for HVAC/plumbing/electrical — missing the highest-intent customers
- Listing services without descriptions — "Plumbing" tells them nothing, "24/7 drain cleaning, water heater replacement, and leak repair" tells them everything

## Same-Niche Rotation System

Build 10 home services sites and they should all look different. Pick the variant that matches the business personality, and rotate so consecutive same-niche builds use different variants.

| | **Variant A: Trusted Pro / Clean Authority** | **Variant B: Dark Industrial / Emergency Power** | **Variant C: Warm Family / Community Roots** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hero | Bright white hero with professional blue accent, split-screen with technician photo on one side and trust stats (years, rating, "Licensed & Insured") on the other, clean authoritative feel | Dark slate hero with bold red or amber accent, full-screen action shot with dark overlay, emergency messaging prominent ("24/7 Service — Call Now"), industrial urgency | Warm cream hero with earth-tone palette, family-owned messaging front and center, owner/team photo, "Keeping [City] Homes Comfortable Since [Year]" tagline, approachable and personal |
| Palette | Palette A (Professional Blue — white + cool grey + trust blue + action orange) or custom clean white + deep blue + green accent | Palette C (Dark Industrial — dark slate + charcoal + bold red + amber) or Obsidian adapted with red/amber accents | Custom warm palette — warm cream bg + sage or warm brown accent + soft orange secondary, or Toasted Almond from color-palettes.md |
| Texture | None — ultra-clean with strong grid structure, thin blue horizontal rules between sections, certification badges in precision layout | Dark noise grain on hero and emergency sections, bold diagonal stripe accents, industrial metal texture on dark cards, high-contrast section transitions | Warm linen noise on alternating sections, rounded section dividers, soft warm shadows, hand-drawn-style service icons |
| Cards | Clean white service cards with blue left border, professional iconography, clear service descriptions, "Schedule Service" CTA per card | Dark charcoal service cards with red/amber accent top border, condensed uppercase service category names, emergency vs. scheduled service tags | Rounded warm cards with sage or earth accent border, friendly service descriptions with "What to Expect" blurbs, warm iconography |
| CTA style | Solid blue button with clean white text, professional copy ("Schedule Your Free Estimate") | Bold red emergency button with pulse animation for urgent CTAs, amber button for standard ("Call Now — Available 24/7") | Warm rounded button in earth tone or sage, friendly copy ("Let's Fix That — Free Estimate") |
| Special | Interactive service category tabs with clean transitions, certification and brand partner logo grid, animated trust counter (homes served, years, rating) | Emergency service banner sticky at top with phone number and red pulse, dark full-width "Why Call Us at 3AM" section, equipment/brand showcase on dark rail | Owner story section with real family/team photos, neighborhood service area map with warm markers, seasonal maintenance tips section, community involvement callout |
| Font mood | Professional clean (Montserrat or Outfit + DM Sans) | Industrial bold (Bebas Neue or Oswald + Open Sans) | Friendly approachable (Nunito or Rubik + Lato) |

**How to pick:** Match variant to business personality. A professional, certification-focused operation emphasizing reliability and clean work → Variant A. An emergency-driven service (HVAC breakdown, burst pipe, electrical emergency) with 24/7 availability and industrial capability → Variant B. A family-owned, community-rooted business emphasizing personal relationships and neighborhood trust → Variant C. Document which variant in App.jsx header comment.
